<p><p>Styrene-butadiene rubber (SBR) is a synthetic rubber used widely in various applications, particularly in tires. The market is divided into two main types: emulsion SBR (E-SBR) and solution SBR (S-SBR). E-SBR is produced via emulsion polymerization, resulting in a rubber with improved processing characteristics and lower cost, making it suitable for general tire manufacturing. S-SBR, produced through solution polymerization, offers better performance properties, such as enhanced wear resistance and lower rolling resistance, making it ideal for premium tires.</p></p>
