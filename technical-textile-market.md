<p><strong>The Technical Textile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural fiber</li><li>Synthetic polymer</li><li>Regenerated fiber</li><li>Mineral</li><li>Metal</li><li>Specialty fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The technical textile market comprises various fiber types: </p><p>1. **Natural Fiber** - Derived from plants or animals, these fibers are biodegradable and eco-friendly, used in applications like automotive and medical textiles.  </p><p>2. **Synthetic Polymer** - Engineered from petrochemicals, these fibers offer durability and versatility, commonly found in construction and protective clothing.  </p><p>3. **Regenerated Fiber** - Made by chemically processing natural materials, these fibers combine sustainability with performance, often used in fashion and home textiles.  </p><p>4. **Mineral Fiber** - Comprised of inorganic materials, these fibers are heat-resistant and used in insulation and fireproof applications.  </p><p>5. **Metal Fiber** - Incorporating metallic elements, these fibers provide conductivity and are applied in electronics and specialized protective garments.  </p><p>6. **Specialty Fiber** - Encompassing unique materials tailored for specific applications, they include aramid and carbon fibers utilized in aerospace and automotive sectors.</p></p>
