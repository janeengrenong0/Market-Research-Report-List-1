<p><p>The electric supercar market is categorized into three main types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV), and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV). BEVs are fully electric, relying solely on batteries for power, offering zero emissions and high performance. PHEVs combine a conventional engine with an electric motor, allowing for both electric-only and hybrid driving. HEVs utilize a combination of an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, improving fuel efficiency without needing external charging. Each type caters to diverse consumer preferences for performance and environmental impact.</p></p>
