<p><ul><li>Indirect Liquid Cooling System</li><li>Direct Liquid Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Liquid Cooling System market comprises two main types: Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems and Direct Liquid Cooling Systems. Indirect Liquid Cooling Systems use a heat exchanger to transfer heat from the data center environment to a secondary fluid, minimizing direct contact with IT equipment. In contrast, Direct Liquid Cooling Systems involve circulating coolant directly to the components, enhancing heat dissipation efficiency. Both systems aim to improve energy efficiency, reduce operational costs, and allow for higher-density computing setups in data centers.</p></p>
