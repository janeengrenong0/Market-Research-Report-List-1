<p><p>The stationary Li-ion battery market is categorized into three main types: Li-Ni (Lithium Nickel), Li-Ni-Co (Lithium Nickel Cobalt), and Li-Mn (Lithium Manganese). Li-Ni batteries are known for high energy density and lower cost, making them suitable for large-scale storage. Li-Ni-Co batteries offer enhanced thermal stability and longer life, ideal for applications requiring reliability. Li-Mn batteries deliver excellent thermal stability and safety, making them a popular choice for renewable energy integration and backup power systems. Each type contributes uniquely to energy storage solutions.</p></p>
