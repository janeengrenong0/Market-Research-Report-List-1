<p><strong>The Commercial Toilet Tank Fill Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Ball</li><li>Float Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial toilet tank fill valve market is primarily segmented into two types: float ball and float cup mechanisms. Float ball valves utilize a buoyant ball attached to a lever that rises with water, shutting off the flow when the desired level is reached. Conversely, float cup valves employ a cylindrical float that moves up and down within a guide to regulate water flow. Both types effectively control tank refilling, ensuring optimal water levels and efficient functioning in commercial restroom applications.</p></p>
