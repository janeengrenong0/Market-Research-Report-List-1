<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ship Exhaust Gas Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ship Exhaust Gas Cleaning System market comprises three primary types: open loop scrubbers, closed loop scrubbers, and hybrid scrubbers. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to remove sulfur oxides from exhaust gases, discharging the treated water back into the ocean. Closed loop scrubbers circulate a chemical solution to neutralize emissions, disposing of waste responsibly. Hybrid scrubbers offer flexibility, operating in both open and closed modes, adapting to regulatory requirements and water quality conditions, making them increasingly popular in maritime operations.</p></p>
