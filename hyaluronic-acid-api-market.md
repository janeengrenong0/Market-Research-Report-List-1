<p><p>The Hyaluronic Acid API market is primarily divided into two types: Animal Tissue and Microbial Fermentation. Animal Tissue-derived hyaluronic acid is extracted from the tissues of animals, such as rooster combs or bovine sources, which offers high molecular weight and purity but raises concerns about ethical sourcing and cross-species contamination. In contrast, Microbial Fermentation involves using bacteria to produce hyaluronic acid through fermentation processes, ensuring a sustainable and ethical source, while also allowing for greater control over production conditions and purity.</p></p>
