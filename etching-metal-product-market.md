<p><p>The metal etching market is categorized into two main types: wet etching and dry etching. Wet etching utilizes liquid chemicals to remove material from the metal surface, ideal for achieving intricate designs and smoother finishes. It is widely used for applications like circuit board manufacturing. In contrast, dry etching employs plasma or gas to etch materials, providing greater precision and control, making it suitable for semiconductor fabrication. Both methods are crucial in electronics, automotive, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
