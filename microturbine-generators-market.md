<p><p>The microturbine generators market is primarily divided into two types: unrecuperated microturbines and recuperated microturbines. Unrecuperated microturbines operate without a heat recovery system, resulting in lower efficiency but simpler design and lower costs, making them suitable for various small-scale applications. In contrast, recuperated microturbines utilize a heat exchanger to recover waste heat, enhancing overall efficiency and performance. This makes them ideal for applications requiring higher energy output and better thermal management, often in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
